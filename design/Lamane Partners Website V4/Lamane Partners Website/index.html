Who we Are

Lamane Partners is a law firm rooted in Dakar (Senegal) with a singular focus on West African countries. The firm is dedicated to handling a wide array of African business issues, ranging from corporate/M&A and private equity to infrastructure project and finance transactions.

Lamane Partners tailors its legal strategies to meet the specific needs of its clients operating in diverse sectors. The firm is committed to building long-term relationships with clients. We prioritize understanding their business objectives and providing legal solutions aligned with their strategic goals.

With a deep appreciation for the diverse cultures and legal systems across the African continent, the firm is well-positioned to guide both local and international clients through the intricacies of West African business ventures.

Founder

Thierry embarked on his legal career in 2014 at Clifford Chance Paris where he spent 4 years. He joined Cleary Gottlieb Steen & Hamilton LLP as an associate in 2018. His exceptional talent and dedication quickly set him apart, leading to his appointment as Senior Associate in 2023 at Cleary Gottlieb Steen & Hamilton LLP.

Thierry’s practice focuses on corporate and Africa-related transactions, including mergers, acquisitions, private equity, and projects. He has advised such clients as Alstom, Atlas Trust, the historic shareholders of CGF Bourse, GlobalFoundries, Latour Capital, Loxam, the Republic of Senegal, and Sienna Investment Managers.
